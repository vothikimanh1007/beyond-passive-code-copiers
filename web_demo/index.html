                    Welcome to the interactive verification platform. Follow these step-by-step instructions to experience how the curriculum counters cognitive offloading and leverages psychological safety.
                

                
                
                

                    

                        1
                        Verify Empirical Metrics (Overview Tab)
                    

                    

                        Analyze the interactive SVG charts in the Empirical Overview. These charts plot real quantitative metrics from our N=299 study—such as the 12.8% active refactoring rate and 76.2% accountability improvement.
                    

                


                
                

                    

                        2
                        Run Active Auditing (Auditor Tab)
                    

                    

                        Go to the AI Auditing Auditor tab. Select a database anomaly from the left panel (e.g., O(N²) Loop, Mutating Tables, or Race Conditions). Click "Execute Active Audit" to simulate how students identify and refactor automated bugs, preserving "desirable difficulties".
                    

                


                
                

                    

                        3
                        Run Agile Simulations (Simulator Tab)
                    

                    

                        Switch to Sprint Role Simulator. Choose a student's assigned Agile role (Tech Lead, QA, or Architect) to see how functional boundaries reduce communication overhead. Press "Run Virtual Sprint Game" to watch real-time Scrum sprint metrics calculate dynamically.
                    

                


                
                

                    

                        4
                        Modulate Human-AI Dynamics (Sliders)
                    

                    

                        Adjust the Sycophancy Slider on the sidebar to see how flattery manipulates student trust. Navigate to the Ergonomics Paradox tab and adjust the CLT Slider to see the exact trade-offs between physical constraints (Sweller's CLT) and psychological safe zones (Krashen's Affective Filter).
